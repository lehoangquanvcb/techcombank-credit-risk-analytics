
# Power BI Dashboard Specification

## Page 1: Executive Risk Overview
KPIs: Total EAD, Total ECL, Weighted Average PD, Weighted Average LGD, Stage 2 Ratio, Stage 3/NPL Ratio, Severe Stress ECL Increase.

## Page 2: Portfolio Quality
Visuals: EAD by industry, rating distribution, region concentration, top 20 obligors.

## Page 3: IFRS9 ECL
Visuals: Stage 1/2/3 distribution, ECL by sector, ECL waterfall, provision movement.

## Page 4: Stress Testing
Visuals: Base/Mild/Severe scenario comparison, stressed PD/LGD/ECL, capital impact.

## Page 5: Early Warning
Visuals: Red/Amber/Green watchlist, DPD deterioration, DSCR weakness, leverage stress.

## Page 6: Model Validation
Visuals: AUC, Gini, KS, PSI, calibration table, champion/challenger comparison.

## Page 7: Management Actions
Visuals: sector-level actions, policy tightening, re-pricing, collateral review, provisioning buffer.
